## ğŸ“š What Is the Birthday Paradox?

The **Birthday Paradox** states that in a group of just 23 people, there is about a 50% chance that at least two people share the same birthday.

This project simulates that concept by:

1. Generating random birthdays for a group of people.
2. Checking if at least two people share the same birthday.
3. Running 100,000 simulations.
4. Calculating the probability of a shared birthday.

---

## ğŸ® How The Program Works

### Step 1:
The user enters the number of people in the group (between 2 and 100).

### Step 2:
The program generates random birthdays.

### Step 3:
It checks if at least two birthdays match.

### Step 4:
It runs 100,000 simulations to calculate the probability.
